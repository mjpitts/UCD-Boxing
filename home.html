Welcome to the Boxing Club at UC Davis

  The UC Davis boxing club competes in the National Collegiate Boxing Association (NCBA) Western Division. The team competes primarily against schools in Northern California and Nevada. Open tryouts are held every quarter. Both serious boxers and students just trying to get fit are welcome.

Learn More

Club

  Our club program provides UC Davis students with a difficult, fun, and rewarding workout and a life long hobby. To join the boxing club one does not need the desire to participate in competitions, but instead can just train with the team and participate in sparring if desired.

Competition

  The UC Davis boxing team is widely considered one of the most technical and well rounded teams around. Competing with the boxing team provides students with the oportunity to push themselves both physically and mentally against students from other colleges.

Practice

  Our practice run from 5-7pm in the pavillion where for the first 20 minutes we will run and warm up. This will followed by mitt work and technique and finally sparring for those who are willing and ready.
